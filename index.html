About Us

Cyberland are a family owned and managed Theme Park in the South East of England. Over the years we have grown from a small park with only 12 employees to a nationally recognised place to spend the day attracting over 230,000 visitors every year! We have a reputation for delivering quality entertainment in a safe environment for all ages. We sincerely believe in our motto “Where the fun never stops!”

Apprenticeship

We have a great opportunity for Apprentice Web Developers to join our team. You need to be self motivated, driven, and focussed on the delivery of a varied portfolio of digital projects and ongoing retainers. You can opt to start in January 2023 or Summer 2023. Salary for this role is £12,960. Overview The Apprentice Developer role is an entry level role requiring no previous experience. We will provide full training in all aspects of Web Development through an accredited training provider. You will work on a variety of project related and support tasks, in a small team of experienced developers learning practical skills alongside your academic studies.

Our multi-award winning apprenticeship programme is delivered in two phases:

  • Level 3 Software Development Apprenticeship (Approximately 12-18 months)
  • Level 6 Degree Apprenticeship in either Management Practice or Digital and Technical Solutions (Approximately 3-4 years)
